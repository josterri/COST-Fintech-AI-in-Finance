COST Action CA19130 - Fintech and Artificial Intelligence in Finance

Acknowledgement

This archive is based upon work from COST Action CA19130 - Fintech and Artificial Intelligence in Finance - Towards a transparent financial industry (FinAI), supported by COST (European Cooperation in Science and Technology).

COST (European Cooperation in Science and Technology) is a funding agency for research and innovation networks. COST Actions help connect research initiatives across Europe and enable scientists to grow their ideas by sharing them with their peers.
