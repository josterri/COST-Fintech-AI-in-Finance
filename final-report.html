Executive Summary

A Remarkable Four-Year Journey

The COST Action on Fintech and AI in Finance has brought together an incredibly diverse network and witnessed enormous growth to 420+ interdisciplinary researchers from 55 countries, with 39 of those countries being European COST countries, becoming the second largest and most active COST Action in Europe.

The academic achievements of the network are impressive. The Action organized 150+ research conferences with more than 6,000 participants and 300+ distinguished speakers. The network's publications have been cited more than 10,000 times, demonstrating the significant impact and relevance of the research conducted.

All 8 MoU objectives were achieved at 76-100% level, and all 11 deliverables were successfully delivered, representing a 100% completion rate for the Action.

"The COST Action has established an inclusive community of researchers on methodological and technological themes in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, promoting Early Career Investigators and creating one of the largest research networks on Fintech and AI in Finance in all of Europe."

- Final Achievement Report
