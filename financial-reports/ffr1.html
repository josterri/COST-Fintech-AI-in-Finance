Key Activities

  • Action Launch: Official start of COST Action CA19130 - Fintech and Artificial Intelligence in Finance
  • Working Groups Established: WG1, WG2, and WG3 each launched with 30 initial members
  • 9 STSMs: First wave of Short-Term Scientific Missions enabling researcher mobility
  • COVID-19 Impact: Pandemic restrictions limited in-person activities, leading to lower budget utilization
  • Virtual Networking: Adapted to online collaboration tools and virtual meetings
  • Deliverable D1: Strategy to engage stakeholders completed

Context

Grant Period 1 was significantly impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic. Despite travel restrictions and the shift to virtual collaboration, the Action successfully:

  • Launched all three working groups with strong initial membership
  • Funded 9 STSMs totaling 20,800 EUR and 230 researcher-days
  • Established virtual networking infrastructure
  • Completed the first deliverable on stakeholder engagement

The 75% budget utilization reflects pandemic-related constraints rather than planning issues. The unspent funds were carried forward to support future activities.
