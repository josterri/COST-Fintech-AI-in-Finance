Key Activities

  • Return to In-Person Events: First major in-person workshops since the pandemic began
  • 4 International Workshops: Berlin, Rennes, Zagreb, and Naples hosted successful events
  • 1,032 Participants: Strong attendance across all meetings
  • Diversity Focus: Naples workshop specifically addressed diversity challenges in FinTech
  • Virtual Mobility Pilot: First VM grant in GP2 exploring gender differences in FinTech
  • ITC Engagement: Zagreb meeting in Croatia (ITC country) engaged regional researchers

Meeting Highlights

  • Berlin Workshop (Mar 2022): Machine Learning approaches for Finance and Management - attracted 238 participants
  • Rennes WG1 Meeting (Apr 2022): Working Group 1 focused session with 254 attendees
  • Zagreb TINFIN (May 2022): Largest event with 315 participants; Technology, Innovation and Stability theme
  • Naples Diversity Workshop (May 2022): 225 participants; 13 researchers reimbursed; highest per-meeting expenditure

Context

Grant Period 2 marked a transition period for the COST Action:

  • Shortened period (7 months) due to grant cycle restructuring
  • Resumption of in-person activities after pandemic restrictions lifted
  • No STSMs were awarded in this period as the focus was on re-establishing meeting activities
  • Strong workshop attendance (1,032 total participants) demonstrated continued interest
  • Unspent budget carried forward to GP3 for expanded activities

The 17% utilization rate reflects the shortened period and cautious restart rather than reduced activity. The successful workshops laid groundwork for expanded programming in subsequent periods.
