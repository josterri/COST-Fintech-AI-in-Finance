Context

Grant Period 3 was a transformative 5-month period that marked the Action's transition from pandemic-constrained operations to full-scale activity. Despite being the shortest grant period, it achieved near-complete budget utilization.

Key achievements:

  • Highest STSM count in a single grant period (10)
  • Strong focus on Young Researcher Investigators (70% of STSMs)
  • Excellent ITC country participation (60% of STSMs)
  • Major in-person conferences across Europe
  • Significant working group membership growth

The 98% budget utilization in just 5 months demonstrates exceptional planning and execution during the post-pandemic recovery period.
