Key Activities

  • Peak Activity Year: Highest number of activities in any grant period
  • 8 International Meetings: From Romania to the Azores, spanning all of Europe
  • 4 Training Schools: Albania, Netherlands, Germany, and Italy hosted capacity-building events
  • 49 Trainees: Researchers trained in cutting-edge FinTech and AI methods
  • Brussels Policy Event: Key meeting on policy implications of FinTech and AI
  • Women in FinTech III: Continued commitment to diversity and inclusion
  • 12 Virtual Mobility Grants: Enabled remote collaboration on diverse research topics

Geographic Coverage

Meetings hosted in: Romania, Italy, Belgium, Portugal (2x), Finland, Switzerland, Spain

Training Schools in: Albania, Netherlands, Germany, Italy

ITC Country Engagement: Strong participation from Inclusiveness Target Countries including Romania, Albania, Lithuania, Turkey, Czechia, Portugal, and Poland

Context

Grant Period 4 represented the peak operational phase of the COST Action:

  • Near-complete budget utilization (99.6%) demonstrated excellent planning and execution
  • Meetings exceeded budget allocation, funded by reallocation from other categories
  • Four successful training schools trained 49 researchers in advanced methods
  • Strong ITC engagement with events in Romania, Albania, and participation from 7+ ITC countries
  • Virtual Mobility grants (12 total) enabled continued collaboration during travel gaps
  • Policy engagement in Brussels connected research to regulatory discussions
