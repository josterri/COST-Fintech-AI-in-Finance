Legacy and Impact

COST Action CA19130 "Fintech and Artificial Intelligence in Finance - Towards a transparent financial industry" successfully concluded in September 2024, leaving a lasting impact on the European research landscape:

  • Research Network: Built a network of 743 researchers across 50 countries
  • Knowledge Transfer: Trained 96 PhD students and early-career researchers
  • Scientific Output: 15 deliverables including databases, methodologies, and publications
  • Researcher Mobility: Enabled 27 STSMs with 392 researcher-days of collaboration
  • ITC Capacity Building: Strong engagement from Inclusiveness Target Countries
  • Industry Bridge: Connected academia with fintech and finance industry stakeholders
  • Open Resources: Created open source software and educational materials

The Action's work on transparency in AI-driven finance, explainable models, and investment performance evaluation will continue to influence research and policy in the years ahead.
