Key Observations

  • Budget Utilization Improved Over Time: From 75% in GP1 to 100% in GP5, demonstrating enhanced planning and execution.
  • GP2 Anomaly: The 17% utilization in GP2 was due to the shortened 7-month transition period and COVID-19 restrictions. Funds were carried forward.
  • Meetings Dominated Spending: Nearly half (48.5%) of total expenditure went to organizing 40+ meetings and workshops across Europe.
  • Training Schools Success: 96 PhD students and early-career researchers trained through 7 schools, representing 14% of budget.
  • Strong STSM Program: 27 Short-Term Scientific Missions enabled researcher mobility across 15 countries.
  • Virtual Mobility Adoption: Introduced in GP4 and grew to 15.7% of budget in GP5, adapting to hybrid collaboration models.
