A About

COST Action CA19130 brought together researchers, practitioners, and policymakers from across Europe to advance the understanding and application of artificial intelligence and fintech in the financial sector.

The action ran from October 2020 to October 2024, fostering collaboration through working groups, training schools, short-term scientific missions, and conferences.

Duration: October 2020 - October 2024
Website: www.fintech-ho2020.eu

W Working Groups

WG1: AI in Credit Risk

Machine learning approaches to credit scoring, default prediction, and risk assessment in lending markets.

WG2: AI in Market Microstructure

High-frequency trading, market making algorithms, and price discovery mechanisms.

WG3: Blockchain & Digital Assets

Cryptocurrency markets, DeFi protocols, and distributed ledger applications in finance.

T Timeline
