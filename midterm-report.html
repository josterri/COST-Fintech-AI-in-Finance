Executive Summary

Remarkable Growth and Impact

The COST Action on Fintech and AI in Finance has brought together an incredibly diverse network and witnessed enormous growth to 260 interdisciplinary researchers from 49 countries, with 39 of those countries being European COST countries, becoming one of the largest and most active COST Actions in Europe.

The academic achievements of the network are impressive. The COST Action has organized 150 research conferences with more than 6,000 participants and 300 distinguished speakers. The network's publications have been cited more than 10,000 times, demonstrating the impact and relevance of the research being conducted.

"The FinTech and AI in Finance Action has demonstrably been highly successful in nurturing the field, and supporting the development of a strong community of data scientists, economic and social science experts and practitioners, and a wide array of stakeholders, including regulators and policy-makers from 49 countries."

- Rapporteur Review, External Assessment

Critical Contributions Recognized

Regulatory and Policy Impact: The Action's contribution to involving, informing and educating regulators and policy decision-makers around nascent technological developments in DeFi, cryptocurrencies, decentralised assets and related innovations has been recognized as critically significant.

Long-term Platform Contributions: The establishment of frameworks and platforms for hosting and sharing datasets (Quantlet, Quantinar, Blockchain Research Center) provides workable platforms for future research and analysis, guaranteeing long-term impact.

Explainable AI Leadership: The Action plays a significant role in promoting developments in Explainable and Interpretable AI, crucially important for financial services with reference to end-user privacy, personal data custody and regulatory policy developments in Europe.
