Fintech and Artificial Intelligence in Finance

Towards a transparent financial industry

About the Action

This COST Action brings together researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to address the challenges and opportunities arising from the rapid adoption of artificial intelligence and fintech solutions in the financial industry. Our mission is to promote transparency, fairness, and accountability in financial services through interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge exchange across Europe and beyond.

Working Groups

Working Group 1

Transparency in FinTech

Investigating transparency requirements and solutions in fintech applications, including blockchain, digital payments, and regulatory compliance.

280 Members
Working Group 2

Transparent vs Black Box Models

Comparing transparent and opaque machine learning models in financial decision-making, focusing on explainability and interpretability.

253 Members
Working Group 3

Investment Product Transparency

Main Objectives

1

Research Networking

Build a pan-European research network on AI and fintech transparency.

2

Knowledge Transfer

Facilitate knowledge exchange between academia, industry, and regulators.

3

Early Career Support

Support early-career researchers through training schools and STSMs.

4

Policy Impact

Contribute to evidence-based policy recommendations on financial AI.

5

Best Practices

Develop guidelines for transparent AI in financial services.

6

ITC Inclusiveness

Promote participation from Inclusiveness Target Countries.
