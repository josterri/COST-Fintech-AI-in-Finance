<div class="timeline" markdown>

<div class="timeline-item" markdown>

- 63 Management Committee members
- Grant Holder: Zurich University of Applied Sciences (ZHAW)

<div class="timeline-item" markdown>

- First virtual meetings organized (COVID-19 pandemic)

<div class="timeline-item" markdown>

<div class="timeline-item" markdown>
