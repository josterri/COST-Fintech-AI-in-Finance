## Working Groups

<div class="wg-cards" markdown>

<div class="wg-card" markdown>
<div class="wg-card-header wg1" markdown>
<h3>WG1: Transparency in FinTech</h3>
<span class="wg-leader">Lead: Prof Wolfgang Hardle</span>
</div>
<div class="wg-card-body" markdown>

Investigating transparency in blockchain, cryptocurrencies, NFTs, and digital assets. Research focuses on fraud detection using ML and operational fragility prediction.

<div class="wg-stats">
<span>281 Members</span>
<span>46 Countries</span>
</div>

[Learn more](working-groups/wg1/){ .md-button }

</div>
</div>

<div class="wg-card" markdown>
<div class="wg-card-header wg2" markdown>
<h3>WG2: XAI & Decision Models</h3>
<span class="wg-leader">Lead: Prof Petre Lameski</span>
</div>
<div class="wg-card-body" markdown>

Making black-box models transparent and interpretable through Explainable AI (XAI). Focus on credit risk modeling, fairness in ML, and model robustness.

<div class="wg-stats">
<span>254 Members</span>
<span>40+ Countries</span>
</div>

[Learn more](working-groups/wg2/){ .md-button }

</div>
</div>

<span class="wg-leader">Lead: Prof Peter Schwendner</span>
</div>

## Impact Highlights

!!! success "Research Output"
    - **1,000+ peer-reviewed publications** with 10,000+ citations
    - **15 completed deliverables** including databases, frameworks, and position papers
    - **Multiple open-source software packages** and research datasets

!!! info "Network Growth"
    - Started with **33 countries**, expanded to **55 countries globally**
    - **420 interdisciplinary researchers** from academia and industry
    - **20 PhD completions** and **10 professorships** achieved by members

!!! note "Knowledge Transfer"
    - **52 meetings** including conferences, workshops, and MC meetings
    - **7 training schools** with 96 trainees
    - **27 STSMs** and **39 virtual mobility grants** supporting researcher exchange
