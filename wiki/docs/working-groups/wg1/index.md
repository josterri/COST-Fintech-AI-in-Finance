# WG1: Transparency in FinTech

## Overview

**Leader**: Prof Wolfgang Hardle (Humboldt University Berlin)

**Participants**: 281 researchers from 40+ countries

Investigating transparency in blockchain, cryptocurrencies, NFTs, and digital assets. Research focuses on fraud detection using ML and operational fragility prediction.

## Research Focus

- Blockchain technology and transparency
- Cryptocurrency market analysis
- Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs)
- Decentralized Finance (DeFi)
- NFT markets and valuation
- Fraud detection in digital assets
- Smart contract analysis
- Crypto exchange transparency

## Key Deliverables

- D1: Stakeholder Strategy
- D3: ICO Database
- D4: Crowdfunding Database
- D6: Stress Tests AI/ML
