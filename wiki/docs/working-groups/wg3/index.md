# WG3: Transparency into Investment Product Performance

## Overview

**Leader**: Prof Peter Schwendner (ZHAW Zurich)

**Participants**: 223 researchers from 40+ countries

Evaluating and communicating investment product performance to clients. Research on ESG criteria, sustainable investments, stress testing AI/ML models, and digital asset investments.

## Research Focus

- Investment performance analytics
- ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) metrics
- Sustainable investment evaluation
- Portfolio risk transparency
- Digital asset investment strategies
- Performance attribution analysis
- False discovery rate control
- Factor model transparency

## Key Deliverables

- D5: Backtesting Framework
- D9: Risk Management Handbook
- D10: Digital Asset Risk Position Paper
- D12: AI Models for Network Analysis
