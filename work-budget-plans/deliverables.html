Strategy to engage stakeholders

Due: Month 6 Completed

Comprehensive stakeholder engagement strategy with revisions at months 24 and 36. Identified key industry partners, regulators, and academic institutions for collaboration.

Report on best practices for transparent finance

Due: Month 12 Completed

Report with 12 guidelines on good examples and best practices for transparency in financial services and fintech applications.

3

Database with pre-ICO documentation

Due: Month 24 Completed

Database linking pre-ICO documentation (whitepapers, team information) to post-ICO performance metrics for research analysis.

4

Database with crowdfunding/P2P platform features

Due: Month 24 Completed

Comprehensive database for fraud prediction in crowdfunding and P2P lending platforms, including platform characteristics and failure indicators.

5

Discussion paper on back-testing framework

Due: Month 24 Completed

Statistically valid back-testing framework for investment strategies, addressing common pitfalls and establishing rigorous methodological standards.

6

Internal database of financial time series

Due: Month 24 Completed

Curated data from cryptocurrency exchanges and financial markets for collaborative research purposes within the Action.

7

Discussion papers on ICO/crowdfunding methodology

Due: Month 36 Completed

Methodology for evaluating ICOs and crowdfunding platforms, including risk assessment frameworks and success prediction models.

8

Position papers for regulators on AI testing

Due: Month 36 Completed

Methodology with formal criteria for testing AI techniques in real-time financial applications, aimed at informing regulatory frameworks.

9

Handbook on risk management

Due: Month 36 Completed

Wiki/handbook on risk management for blockchain applications and P2P lending, covering operational, market, and credit risks.

10

Position paper on digital asset risks

Due: Month 48 Completed

Roadmap on mitigating risks with digital assets, including cryptocurrencies, NFTs, and tokenized securities.

11

Methodology on stress tests for AI/ML

Due: Month 48 Completed

Discussion paper on stress testing AI/ML models in financial applications, ensuring robustness under adverse market conditions.

12

AI models for failed trials analysis

Due: Month 48 Completed

Discussion papers on AI models for network data analysis, with applications to understanding failure patterns in financial networks.

13

Four annual reports for lay audience

Due: Month 48 Completed

Annual reports disseminated via media channels, making research accessible to non-specialist audiences and policymakers.

14

Key software/codes/packages

Due: Month 48 Completed

Open source software contributions from all three working groups, available for the research community.

Edited volume on scientific achievements

Due: Month 48 Completed

Comprehensive edited volume documenting Action achievements, including key research findings and collaborative outcomes.
