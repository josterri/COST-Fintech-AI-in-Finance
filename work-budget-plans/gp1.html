Context

Grant Period 1 was significantly impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic, which limited in-person activities. Despite these challenges, the Action successfully launched its three working groups and established virtual networking capabilities. The focus was on building the foundational network and enabling the first wave of Short-Term Scientific Missions.

The lower budget utilization (75%) reflects the pandemic-related restrictions on travel and physical meetings during this period.
