Context

Grant Period 2 was an abbreviated period of only 7 months, serving as a transition between the pandemic-affected GP1 and the more active GP3. The low utilization rate (17%) is primarily due to:

  • Shortened period (Nov 2021 - May 2022)
  • Continued COVID-19 travel restrictions in early 2022
  • Strategic decision to carry budget forward for larger activities
  • No STSMs processed during this transition period

The unspent budget was successfully carried over to GP3, where it enabled the most intensive period of Action activities.
