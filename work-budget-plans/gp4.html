Context

Grant Period 4 represented the maturation of the Action with near-perfect budget utilization (99.6%) and the introduction of training schools. This 12-month period saw the highest total expenditure of any grant period.

Key milestones:

  • Largest budget in Action history (257,926 EUR)
  • First formal training schools launched
  • Virtual Mobility grants introduced as new funding mechanism
  • Strong ITC country engagement through Albania school
  • Increased dissemination and outreach activities
