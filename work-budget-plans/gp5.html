Context

Grant Period 5 marked the successful conclusion of COST Action CA19130, achieving perfect 100% budget utilization. This final period saw the largest training school investment and the completion of all 15 planned deliverables.

The Action successfully:

  • Built a network of 743 members across 3 working groups
  • Engaged 50 countries including strong ITC participation
  • Funded 27 STSMs enabling researcher mobility
  • Trained 98 PhD students and ECRs through 7 training schools
  • Delivered all 15 planned deliverables on schedule
  • Created lasting collaborations in Fintech and AI research
